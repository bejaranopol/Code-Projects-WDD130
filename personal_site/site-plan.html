Purpose

The purpose of my site-plan is to offer sales services such as modern entertainment items, candy and sweet foods, and any item that is related to movies, series or video games.

Audience

My audience is all people interested in entertainment from the internet, television, and video game consoles. These people are between the ages of 13 to 50 years approximately. I chose this population because in my city it follows the trends of modern entertainment

Content

Home page

Introduction
Kazuha's Store is a store where you will find everything related to modern entertainment, we hope you can enjoy your visit today. We have a lot to offer you, so you can take a little walk through our website, we promise that here you will find the product and item you are looking for.

About Us
Our love and fanaticism for movies, anime, series, video games, and sweets motivated us to start this great adventure in 2020 during the pandemic. In those times, people spent a lot of time at home and due to the restrictions it was difficult to find articles of our favorite series or program while it was broadcasting. Therefore, we decided to start this business to bring happiness to fans in the midst of the pandemic.

Our vision
We hope to be one of the best web pages to offer quality content and exclusive products for the city of Huaraz, we always have in mind the satisfaction of our customers, so we hope you can find here everything you need
to satisfy the Fan that is within you.

Our logistics team will be in charge of assisting you and providing you with what you need, we are also fans just like you, so we know how to lend you a hand in what you are looking for ;)
